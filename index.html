Who Uses MedPass?

MedPass is designed for all participants in the healthcare system, ensuring smooth workflow and secure access to patient records:

  • Patients: Access your complete medical history, lab results, and referrals from anywhere.
  • Physicians / Doctors: View patient history, manage prescriptions, and track referrals.
  • Nurses / Triage: Register vitals and manage the clinical intake flow.
  • Lab Technicians: Record and share test results securely with doctors and patients.
  • Accounts / Finance: Handle payments, insurance claims, and generate receipts efficiently.
  • Hospitals & Clinics: Receive referred patients with full medical history for better care coordination.
  • MedPass Admins: Global oversight of system users, hospitals, and security.
